What's Our Story?

and what is the secret behind our catch phrase!

The Story of an Instagram Account turning into a Real Life Shop is old.
But this story is little diffrent, it's about the young Entrepreneur Zaina Johari.

A teen who started out as a Cupcake/Tart Baker on Instagram in early 2014,
She Baked & Worked restlessly and pulled all kinds of "ALL NIGHTERS".
As her Cupcake and Tart Business thrived & gained such popularity,
Zaina started planing for the future ahead and as of June of 2019,
She officially announced her brand new project "Aletta Cafe".

A Business Place she Baked it herself where she's her own BOSS,
and a leader to many girls who walk by her side throughout her Journey.
